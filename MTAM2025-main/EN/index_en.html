Discover Inclusive Spots

Explore the best places in Brasília for everyone! Our mission is to connect you to accessible and welcoming experiences.

Routes in Brasília



Bars

Mimo Bar: Casa MimoBar is an establishment known for being an LGBT bar, where drinks, special beers and several tasty snacks with elements of the cerrado are sold. The bar also has a very rich cultural program; once a month there is a night of Latin music. Casa mimobar works from Tuesday to Sunday.

Barito Bar: Barito Bar and Café is known for its modern, trendy atmosphere inspired by New York’s secret bars, with an underground touch. The space showcases curated art pieces and offers a differentiated musical experience, including electronic and Arabic music nights.

Cafés

AHA Coffee: AHA coffee specializes in specialty coffees. Which aims to respect and pass on the history of the producers behind each coffee and ensure a unique experience in each cup. AHA café works from Monday to Saturday from 8 am to 7 pm.

Civita Café: Civitá Café is a cozy and charming space, known for combining a pleasant atmosphere with a quality menu. The coffee stands out for its proposal that values both the flavor and the customer experience. Civita also stands out for its menu of cakes and handmade breads. Civitá works every day from 8am to 8pm.

Stores

Brechó do Óculos: This Thrift store has a national and international collection with several types of frames. The store has frames for prescription glasses and sunglasses! They have both famous brands and national brands! The store is open from Monday to Saturday!

Lixomania: Lixo Mania Lixo mania is a vintage shop focused on pieces from the 60s to the 2000s, and within the curatorship you can find pieces up to the 20s! Open from Monday to Saturday, Lixomania is a very special place that everyone is welcome!

Cultural Events

MUTA Cultural: MUTA is a Brazilian art event. Muta comes from mutation. Of the desire to make the project this agent of social change. Both in the part of intervention of spaces, and for the revelation of artists from peripheries. For more information about the next editions of the event, keep an eye on the Instagram of the event @muta.df.

Ao Desapego: Ao desapego is the largest circular fashion event in the Federal District, the event includes both shops and small shops with independent productions. Ao Desapego is an event that encourages diversity and individuality through sustainable fashion. The event takes place quarterly in the pilot plan, for more information about the dates keep an eye on Instagram at @Aodesapego.
